Many streams are offered at Lazar but the main streams offered are core academic, music, language streams like Spanish, Italian, French, and Mandarin, and art streams.


Educational Board School Is Registerd To

The board that this school is registered to is the Montville Township Board of Education


Why You Should Join

Robert R. Lazar Middle School in Montville, NJ, serves grades 6-8, offering a strong academic curriculum with 73% math and 81% reading proficiency. It features a character education advisory program, competitive arts, and STEM-focused extracurriculars like MathCounts, Chamber Choir, and a STEAM Club.
