About Us

At Spice Street, we bring the exciting flavors of Indian street food to everyone. Our recipes are inspired by the busy food markets of Mumbai, Delhi, Kolkata, and Ahmedabad, where every dish is prepared with fresh ingredients and bold spices.

Every meal is cooked fresh when you order it. We use homemade spice blends, fresh vegetables, tender meats, and traditional cooking methods to create food that tastes just like it does on the streets of India.

Whether you're craving something crispy, spicy, sweet, or refreshing, our menu has something for everyone. Our goal is to create a fun and welcoming place where families and friends can enjoy authentic Indian street food together.
